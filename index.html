This isn't a thank you...
It's rather a commitment to you that Roark will explore that world in a unique way. We won't tell stories about the most monumental expeditions of the decade, or trivial ones that are wasted breath. If we do our job, the chase for our old mate Roark will inspire you to adventure - and we'll outfit the journey with purpose-driven product built by our travels. We appreciate your support and will do our best to reciprocate.
Safe travels,
